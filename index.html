ALEXANDER BERCOW

Work by Joachim Czichon via Artsy/Cebera Gallery

Alexander is a one-man support department at Artsy, the premier place to buy artworks online. Leading the reactive Partner Support team, Alexander assists Tier 1 to Tier 4 art galleries optimize their Artsy experience and troubleshoot technical escalations. Tasked with creating the first service department at Artsy, Alexander joined Artsy in May 2018. Artsy Partner Support now boasts a proud 10 minute SLA and a 98% customer satisfaction month-over-month. His background in Squarespace's Customer Operations established Alexander's quality-first support style.

Alexander studied electronic music and video game audio at Berklee College of Music. He began his career touring musician and entrepreneur. He followed his passion to Blackedoutand Bandpass, a music tech company Alexander founded through Berklee's ICE Institue with IDEO. They grew into a team of 5 before ultimately dissolving in 2016.
